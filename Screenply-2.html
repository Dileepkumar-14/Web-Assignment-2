Screenplay-Writing course detailes:

Screenplay-writing course is a specialized program designed to teach participants the art and craft of writing scripts for film, television, or digital media. These courses cater to beginners and aspiring professionals, offering a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical exercises. Key topics covered include:
  1. Understanding Screenplay Structure
  2. Crafting Engaging Characters
  3. Creating a Strong Concept
  4. Building a Plot
  5. Writing Effective Dialogue
  6. Scene Composition
  7. Genre and Audience
  8. Formatting and Industry Standards
  9. Pitching and Selling Scripts
  10. Editing and Rewriting
  11. Exploring Different Mediums
  12. Workshopping and Feedback
By the end of the course, participants often complete a short screenplay, a polished scene, or the first draft of a full-length script. This course also offer insights into the industry, networking opportunities, and resources for aspiring screenwriters to pursue their careers.
