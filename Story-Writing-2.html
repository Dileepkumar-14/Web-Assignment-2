Story-Writing course detailes:

Story-writing course is a structured program designed to teach participants the skills and techniques required to craft compelling and engaging stories. Whether aimed at beginners or seasoned writers, such a course typically covers the fundamental elements of storytelling, including:
  1. Understanding Story Structure
  2. Developing Characters
  3. Crafting a Plot
  4. Setting and World-Building
  5. Dialogue Writing
  6. Themes and Symbolism
  7. Point of View (POV) and Perspective
  8. Editing and Revising
  9. Overcoming Writer's Block
  10. Genre Exploration
This course also include interactive elements like group discussions, peer reviews, and assignments to practice writing skills. Advanced programs may delve into topics such as publishing, pitching to agents, or writing for specific mediums like screenplays or short stories.
By the end of the course, participants often walk away with a completed short story or the foundation for a larger project, along with the tools and confidence to continue their writing journey.
